## Q1: As an international student with an undergraduate background in Business (Marketing), do I meet the admission requirements for the UTS Master of Information Technology?

**Timestamp:** 2026-05-31 08:45:34
**Knowledge Base:** Curated markdown knowledge base (UTS_Master_of_IT.md, UNSW_Master_of_IT.md)

**System Response:**
Yes, an undergraduate degree in Business (Marketing) meets the admission requirements for the UTS Master of Information Technology.

The UTS Master of Information Technology accepts applicants with a bachelor's degree in any field of study, including Business (Marketing), as long as they meet one of the following conditions:
1. Completed an Australian master's or doctoral degree (or overseas equivalent), or  
2. Completed an Australian bachelor's degree, graduate certificate, or graduate diploma with at least 75% of subjects passed at a pass level.

Importantly, no prerequisite IT subjects are required, meaning applicants from non-IT backgrounds such as Business (Marketing) are eligible.

If you do not meet these conditions, you may consider applying first for the Graduate Certificate in Information Technology Studies (C11247), which allows up to 24 credit points of Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) to be transferred into the Master of Information Technology.
